Watching Jackie Chan Dance To ‘Tunak Tunak Tun’ Is Giving Us Some Serious 90s Nostalgia

by

Supriyo Mukherjee

835
SHARES

Jackie Chan has been kung-fu fighting and he's fast as lightning. From playing Chief Yan Naing Lee in Rush Hour  to Mr. Miyagi in The Karate Kid, Jackie Chan has won our hearts with everything he's done. Jackie Chan is a huge part of our 90's nostalgia that seems to never fade away. 

Do you know what other thing forms a large chunk of that nostalgia? Tunak tunak tun  by Daler Mehndi. In something right out of our dream sequences, we finally have the combination of Jackie Chan and Tunak tunak tun. YES GUYS, THAT HAPPENED!

Sonu Sood tweeted about this as well. Take a look at him shaking what his mom gave him to the tune of our beloved favourite:

 

All aboard the trip down the nostalgia lane.
